
--- Baseline Model Sample Outputs ---

[Example 1]
Prompt: John went to the store.
Ground Truth: John visited the store to purchase milk. Upon arriving home, he discovered his keys were missing. Remembering he might have left them at the store, he returned to find them.
Baseline Output: John went to the store. He bought milk. Then he went home. He forgot his keys. He went back to store. (Repetitive and lacks causal link)

[Example 2]
Prompt: The cat jumped onto the table.
Ground Truth: The cat jumped onto the table, knocking over a vase. The vase shattered on the floor, making a loud noise. The cat immediately ran and hid under the bed.
Baseline Output: The cat jumped onto the table. It broke the vase. The cat ran away. It was scared. (Simplistic and misses key details)
